# AI Cost Management Is an Architecture Problem

## Draft
Most teams treat AI cost as a finance issue. By the time finance sees the spike, engineering already made the architecture decision that caused it.

In enterprise SaaS, AI cost management should be owned where it starts: system design.

## Key points
- Instrument cost by feature/workflow/tenant
- Route models by complexity/risk
- Manage context as a scarce resource
- Add loop budgets and stop conditions
- Include cost baseline in definition of done
