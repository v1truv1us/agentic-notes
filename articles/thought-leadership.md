# Thought Leadership for Architects

## Draft
Thought leadership for engineers isn't content marketing — it's architecture transparency.

Strong posts show:
1. Context
2. Constraints
3. Decision
4. Result
5. Transferable lesson

## Weekly system
- Capture 1 real decision
- Write 5 bullets (context, constraints, choice, impact, lesson)
- Convert monthly into one long post + short derivatives

## CTA
What's one architecture decision you changed your mind on this year?
